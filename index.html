The best electronic prototyping platform!

evive is an open-source embedded platform for all age groups to help them learn, build, debug their robotics, embedded and other projects. With an Arduino Mega at it's heart, evive offers a unique menu-based visual interface which removes the need to re-program the Arduino repeatedly. evive offers the world of IoT, with power supplies, sensory and actuator support in one small portable unit.

The Magic Lid

Magic lid allows you to use Arduino shields, access GPIO & communication

Power Supply

evive offers multiple power outputs- a 3.3V, a 5V and a potentiometer controlled variable output

Plug and Play Hardware Interface

Control motors, relays, steppers, servos, motor drivers, pneumatics using evive's inbuilt motor driver

Arduino at Heart

evive is powered by Arduino MEGA 2560

Visual Interface

Take control of evive using onscreen menu, device monitoring & plotting facilities

Sense | Plot | Log

24-bit ADCs based precise voltage & current sensing channels, onscreen plotting options & data logging with SD Card

Smart Door Lock with evive

Wirelessly transmit pictures of people who knock your door and unlock the door with your smartphone!

Robotic Manipulator Control

Control hardware with evive's switches, knobs etc., and use buzzer to alert violation of servo limits!

evive's Oscilloscope

Mini dual-channel oscilloscpe for ±30V and upto 3A range with 24-bit isolated ADC
