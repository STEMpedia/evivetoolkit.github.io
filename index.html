Are you a

Hobbyist

With evive, you've got all the components you need in a self-powered portable unit! Reduce your protoyping time and cost.

Student

Make learning easy and fun! Evive provides a complete interface with sensory inputs, actuator controls and AV feedback. Say goodbye to entangled wires.

Educator

Evive makes teaching easy and effective, giving you loads of teaching modules in one small unit. The multiple feedback mechanisms allow easy debugging of your students' projects. evive works with graphical programming environments like Scratch, transforming learning into an art.

Professional

evive is the perfect bridge between exhaustive equipment like DAQs and elementary equipment like DMMs. Do all your beta testing and data logging with evive using its convenient LabVIEW and Python interfaces.
